Hoi An Lantern Making Tour is a great half-day trip to explore Hoi An ancient town highlights but also experience how to make Hoi An lanterns and learn more about local culture, and customs.

Details

As you know, the lantern is a perfect souvenir to take home from Hoi An. Hanging lanterns in front of your house will bring good fortune and happiness to the family. Taking part in a Lantern Making Class and adding a touch of your own personal flair to an oriental ornament is the ideal way to learn more about Hoi An tradition and Vietnamese culture.

Experience

We will enter a local family who is the owner of a lantern-making workshop.
After all, you will be free to explore Hoi An old town or walk back to your hotel by yourself. We finish Hoi An lantern-making tour.
